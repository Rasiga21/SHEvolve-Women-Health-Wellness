# SHEvolve – Empowering Women's Health & Wellness

SHEvolve is a user-centered digital platform designed to empower women by providing access to health resources, educational content, career support, community engagement, and safety tools in one inclusive space.

## Problem Statement
Women face challenges in accessing reliable health information, career guidance, financial literacy, and safe support systems in a single platform. SHEvolve aims to address these issues through an inclusive and accessible digital solution.

## Features
- Women's Health Hub (menstrual, mental, and general wellness)
- Educational resources on women’s rights, career growth, and financial literacy
- Health and fitness tracking (conceptual)
- Career and education support
- Community discussion and peer support
- Gender-based violence and workplace harassment support
- Financial empowerment resources
- Inspirational stories of women achievers
